Women

Women's clothing are pieces of attire worn by women such as Theresa in Kingdom Come: Deliverance. Henry can purchase or steal these items but, being male, and given the dim view of cross-dressing in Medieval Europe, he cannot wear them - he wears armour.

Men

Men's Wear is a 1953 painting by Australian artist John Brack. The painting depicts the interior of a menswear store, the proprietor and some mannequins, standing in front of displays of ties and trousers. A mirror in the background reflects a silhouette of the artist.[1]

Kids

Children's clothing or kids' clothing is clothing for children who have not yet grown to full height. Children's clothing is often more casual than adult clothing, fit for play and rest.
